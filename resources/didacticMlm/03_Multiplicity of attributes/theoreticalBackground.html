2. Theoretical Background:

Similar to association, each attribute must specify a multiplicity. The multiplicity of an attribute specifies the number of values that may be or must be entered for its slots. In UML++, the default multiplicity of an attribute is [0â€¦*], i.e., any number of slot values may be entered. If the maximum multiplicity of an attribute is higher than one, a sequence of values may be entered. This corresponds to known data structures like arrays or lists that contain multiple values.
