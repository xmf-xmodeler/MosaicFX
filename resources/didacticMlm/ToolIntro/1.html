1. Movie Class Example

Okay, let us begin with a simple example: A local cinema wants to manage its ticket selling system.

Create a class that serves to represent movies. Per convention, classes always begin with an uppercase letter and are in the singular form. Hence, name the class “Movie” and not “movies”.


How To Add A Class

To add a class, you can either right-click on the canvas and select 'Add' and then 'Class' or click on 'Class' in the left pane and then on the canvas. In both cases, a window will open where you can define your class.

In the first case, you need to click on the canvas again after closing the window.
