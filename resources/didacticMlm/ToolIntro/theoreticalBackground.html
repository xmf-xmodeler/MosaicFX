UML++ is different to regular UML in many regards. Detailed information and links to publication on the topic can be accessed on the UML-MX web page.

You can always open the web page by clicking on "Help > Get Information on UML-MX" in the Control Center and you can access online tutorials by clicking on "Help > Open Online Tutorials" in the Modeling Editor

The example model you can open here corresponds to the diagram you should receive at the end of the introduction.
